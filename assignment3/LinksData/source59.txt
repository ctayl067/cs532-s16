<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://sasukokenji.blogspot.com/2016/02/20160218-exactly-what-my-instincts-said.html'>[20160218] Exactly what my instincts said to me... ARE TOTALLY TRUE & REAL.</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-107878615957047605' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-h6f5T5gfqi8/VsWmTWPdwxI/AAAAAAAADYI/gqCry5RGJno/s1600/tumblr_inline_o1vtihEysM1tnp4r2_540.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-h6f5T5gfqi8/VsWmTWPdwxI/AAAAAAAADYI/gqCry5RGJno/s1600/tumblr_inline_o1vtihEysM1tnp4r2_540.png" /></a></div>
<br />
What... what the flower you want?<br />
<br />
I've guessed much, and all of them ended to be true. Like... ugh. My life has been always pitiful.<br />
<br />
And I hate to go back home. I don't want to stay at home because I could feel stresses and I'm feeling so down when I'm at home. Feels like... I wanna run away? Anywhere but far from home. Anywhere.<br />
<br />
Being a very nice and good girl isn't me, to be honest. ahh lantaklah broken English sekalipun.<br />
<br />
I don't wanna blame anyone but I'm too upset and frustrated with <i>these </i>matters. I should be rational and think before I act. But, nah, this is unfair. So heart-breaking! Why do I had to live in this mufu'in land called Malaysia?? Ughh... please. History repeats itself (or maybe it doesn't changed at all).<br />
<br />
Since January 2016, I've lost most of my hopes, energy, goals... and I couldn't see the road that I'm taking for. I'm afraid. Worried if I chose the wrong path (bcuz you know this Malaysia land have lost its sights and we're upside down).<br />
<br />
Maybe I can overcome this soon. Please give me more hopes.<br />
<br />
Even though I can score a few subjects for exam (at least 5 subjects), I'm not sure if I can survive with it. Duh...<br />
<br />
Only fools fall for our latest ** I really don't like him and I really want to kick him outta this country.<br />
<br />
AND SITTING AT HOME JUST MAKING ME STRESSED WITH MY MOM I DUNNO BUT I DON'T LIKE EVERY SINGLE THING THAT MY MOM SAID BECAUSE IT HURTS MY HEART SO THAT'S WHY I TRY THE BEST TO NOT GETTING CLOSE OR TALK OR HAVE A TOPIC TO TALK WITH BECAUSE SHE WILL PROBABLY TEASING ME WHICH IS REALLY ANNOYING AND HURT and it feels like she's insulting me all the time with that 'jokes'. My (personal) life is already pitiful and she make it even worst if I ever talked to her about my problems and she won' give me encouragement but making me even more down. ot to forget that she's the main reason why I became so annoying when I talked with <i>them</i>. <i>They </i>said that my voice are really annoying and I acted like a... <i>gedik </i>but when I kept quiet I just realize that my mom had being that way and me, dunno when, follows her style of talking. LIKE WTF!!!! I'm not like that before!!!!! If you don't believe me ask my friends and my siblings!<br />
<br />
I'm so upset with everything and everything seems so wrong with me.<br />
<br />
DID MY MOM HATE CATS/KITTENS? THAT'S SO CRUEL!! Stop complaining about <i>us </i>watching a cat documentary or stories or drooled over some cats around us BECAUSE CATS ARE INCREDIBLY CUTER THAN YOU. I had an allergy to feathers and dusts too but I still tend to like them like wtf mom please don't judge the things that I like. Liking cats is not bad, you know? AT LEAST &nbsp;I DIDN'T RUN AWAY FROM HOUSE BECAUSE I HAD ALWAYS WANTED TO DO THAT BUT THANKS GOD I WAS STILL ON MY &nbsp;RIGHT MIND SO I STAY AND LOCKED MYSELF IN MY BEDROOM.<br />
<br />
arghh my heart is burning rn.<br />
<br />
I'm not hating her but I'm just... unsatisfied. How can they be close to their mom? I can't even. I don't think there's chemistry running between us at all. Both of us have a totally different interests, perangai, etc. If she's not my mom, I will ended up hating her and she'll be my enemy rn.<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
SORRY. OKAY, SORRY. I'M NOT HATING, BUT I WAS UPSET.<br />
<br />
<br />
Please slap my face to wake me up from this immature side of myself. Thank you.<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
P/s: I'm so disappointed. and I've been disappointed for so many times.

<h2 class='date-header'><span>Selasa, 2 Februari 2016</span></h2>

          <div class="date-posts">
        
<div class='post-outer'>
<div class='post hentry uncustomized-post-template' itemprop='blogPost' itemscope='itemscope' itemtype='http://schema.org/BlogPosting'>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://sasukokenji.blogspot.com/2016/02/20160202-just-some-stupid-confession.html'>[20160202] Just Some Stupid Confession.</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-4064927583490485154' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<b>me</b>: *<i>pats jk's shoulder</i>*<br />
<br />
I know how you feel, bro. Literally the same. We're same. The only different is, there's still no one who scold me to make me wake from my <strike>dream</strike> sleep, and no one would read my letter/post like tbh AM I A GHOST TO YOU?? I think I'm a wall or a non-existence creature in their life. If I need some advice or suggestions... even my parents... ughh!!<br />
<br />
They come to me and saw me doing something, and they said "wow, very good~" "awesome" when you clearly know that it's not that great or perfect (nothing is perfect, tho)... they only says good thing in front of you to please you (maybe because they liked you or maybe you're a son/daughter of a wealthy family or whatever).<br />
<br />
I don't know. Maybe I'm a bit emotional today.<br />
<br />
But Jk's recent fancafe update just... can I say that it was accurate?? His post just hits me hard and at the same time, I'm agree with him. If you want us to scold you, I bet some won't. Nobody is perfect, but you can improve yourself to be better; not perfect. What have been you thinking in your mind right now recently? If you ask me, I thought about a lot of things, actually (but nobody will ask me, only me want to ask you).<br />
<br />

Light Light Light Light Light...<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-vFWO-ZG0THs/VkLsYqX494I/AAAAAAAACzg/6XX0NhRkFMI/s1600/exo-statwars-3.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="272" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-vFWO-ZG0THs/VkLsYqX494I/AAAAAAAACzg/6XX0NhRkFMI/s640/exo-statwars-3.gif" width="640" /></a></div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
<b><u><span style="font-size: x-large;">[REVIEW] EXO - LIGHTSABER</span></u></b></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-48Dg9DOQN3Y/VkLlUt28XOI/AAAAAAAACzQ/YZKn8RuBxY4/s1600/cover.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="640" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-48Dg9DOQN3Y/VkLlUt28XOI/AAAAAAAACzQ/YZKn8RuBxY4/s640/cover.jpg" width="640" /></a></div>
<b>[SINGLE] EXO - LIGHTSABER</b><br />
<b>Release Date:</b> 2015.11.11<br />
<b>Genre:</b> Dance<br />
<b>My Ratings:</b>&nbsp;* * * *<br />
<b>Download Link (MP3):&nbsp;</b><span style="font-family: inherit;"><a href="http://www.mediafire.com/download/dudtb16fbv8tdee/01.+LIGHTSABER.mp3">http://www.mediafire.com/download/dudtb16fbv8tdee/01.+LIGHTSABER.mp3</a></span><br />
<br />
OH MY GOD! THIS SONG IS PERFECTION! The beat, I've never heard it from EXO! I would like if they release a song like this next time! I didn't expect anything LIKE THIS for the collaboration (sorry) but look at this! I just watched the teaser an hour before it was released, no knowing when the actual release date. An hour after it released, I look back at their YouTube Channel and found the MV and I calmly click at it and BOOM!!! For my amazement, I'd never expect the song would turn out this good! Oh wait, what did you expect from EXO, anyway? They're always went viral and their songs are always on the top of the charts. I hope they'll keep the success until FOREVER!<br />
<br />
Maybe I didn't expect anything because of their JAPANESE single album. Well, everyone have their own opinion. Ok, nvm.<br />
<br />
What disappoints me is XIUMIN ONLY GOT ONE LINE. THE FREAKING ONE LINE. That 'Lightsaber!' before the 1st chorus. His voice made me going crazy (in a good way) and I was excited to hear his other part but.... WHY?! His solo song 'You Are The One' really good and his voice is quiet good. He should shine more in other EXO songs in the future. XIUMIN, I really looking forward for you.<br />
The other thing is, the song is only for 2:27 minutes and it's too short! Even shorter than BIGBANG's BAE BAE! What... what... what's this? And the music video too, only Kai, Sehun and BAekhyun featured in it. BUT DAMN THEY SLAYED IT! Is that Sehun who rode the motorcycle?? Sehun, you've grown (of course, plus that he's 21 now). Somehow, I forgot that they've once a 12-member group. Damn, 9 is enough! Bless the remaining, loyal members! Not that I hate the former member, but... it's all the past so let's MOVE ON.<br />
<br />
I'm happy that Sehun got some lines (is that even his voice? OMG so deep, so *kai~~)<br />
And Chanyeol's fast rap! Their talents are shining! I'm happy for them! Please, please, please continue that~!!<br />
<br />
NOTE:<br />
*kai: perfect. It's my own dictionary. Hahaha~~<br />
<br />
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
<b><u><span style="font-size: x-large;">CRYING OVER AN ARTICLE.</span></u></b></div>
<br />
Ok. This might sounds weird, but yeah... I cried. I cried and I couldn't even read the article till the end. Is it an article or interview? Hahaha... but I think it's an interview article.<br />
<br />
An interview from E-POP Malay, 68th Issues (May 2015). Page 20-21.<br />
<br />
Now I clearly know since why I'm looking forward on RAP MONSTER even though I'm not an ARMY yet that time. Shockingly, I've read that before and I clearly couldn't remember anything (probably because I'm going crazy over EXO as the main focus in that magazine).<br />
<br />
I already know about his mixtape album that time but I forgot to download it (or try to listen to it). :(<br />
Fool me, fool me. I only remember about it on July... downloaded in on 23rd July 2015.<br />
And his solo Music Videos, I thought it was last year's. But I figured out that it was this year's, in March. I'm too lazy to read the description. I can't believe that I've watched it several times as I watch another BTS' music videos (still not an ARMY yet that time) (uhh... but I didn't watch till the end the 'DO YOU' MV because it was a bit... you know... 'dangerous'). I thought "Cool~" "Wow~ His rap is perfectly awesome!" "This is my type" without even know what did he exactly says.<br />
<br />
Yup, that's true. I've known BTS since their debut. The first BTS performance that I've watched is No More Dream/We are Bulletproof Pt.2 (I don't even remember which one but I think it's No More Dream bcuz of the abs). After being an ARMY, watching their BANGTAN BOMBS, then finally found their debut MVs, and I thought "Hey, I think I've seen this dance and song on TV before but... when?". And finally I remembered it was 2 years ago when I saw them on TV. I thought "Not bad. This song is good, but I've never heard this group name at all before" and "Ah! That dance (showing abs) reminds me of BIGBANG's LA LA LA dance!", but anyway... I quickly grabbed a pen and a paper and wrote down their GROUP NAME and the song name; to download the song, of course. But I don't know, I suddenly forgot about them and focusing on BIGBANG.<br />
<br />
It's pitiful that I even saw them on KBSWorld, they're having a talk idk but I saw their NO MORE DREAM m/v (cutted ver) and it was when they're promoting their DARK&amp;WILD album, if I'm not mistaken (or SKOOL LUV AFFAIR album). That first impression, that (No More Dream) MV (when Jin get out from the bus), I thought that I've seen that somewhere but I don't remember. I can only recognize Jungkook, Suga, Jimin's face that time (not clearly remember the names but I still remember how they looks like that time, since KBS repeated the same clip from BTS after a few dramas/shows). Without even remembering that IT WAS THEM FROM LAST YEAR, I gave some interest on the group. The concept attracted me, the preview of their new song made me wanna hear more (but I don't remember which song it is). And once again, I forgot about them. Hahaha... It's actually not easy for me to stan a group.<br />
<br />
Then, last year, around July or August like that, my friend gave me a compilation videos of funny moments (incl. BTOB, BEAST, EXO, BTS, ect. I don't remember well since the video was ACCIDENTALLY deleted permanently on my pendrive). The video is hilariously funny and entertaining, but that one group, that one video, THAT TWO MEMBERS OF THE MALE GROUP KISSING ON THE LIPS really kills me and it shocks me too much and in my mind I don't think I will ever like THAT group at all (even though I found it funny af). Even though I found it creepy, I kept hitting the play button because I was addicted to the song in that video. It says... "Cuz we got fire, fire, fire! And higher, higher, higher!". The raps and the rhythm, it attracts me so much. I listen to it several times in a day. Really, really addicted to that song. So bad that I don't know that song name that time. Maybe because I was attracted more to D.O. &amp; EXO that time, I don't even realize that it was BTS (plus that they dressed weirdly in that video. Hahaha!!).<br />
<br />
I went crazily over EXO and GD X TAEYANG during the school holiday. I downloaded a lot of songs from them including Luhan and Kris' solo songs.<br />
<br />
In the early of January, I finally decided to try to listen to other group's songs. I found the download link for the 'DANGER' MV, and I decided to download it. But I clicked the wrong link, and I downloaded the 'DANGER' MV, from TAEMIN!! I'm too lazy to re-download the right MV, and search up for another K-POP songs from the internet instead, but download nothing since the internet connection is lost.<br />
<br />
I've heard a lot about BTS, and one day I decided to try to hear one of their song (I don't know what's their song tittle so I just search 'BTS MP3' on the search engine) and finally found their songs on 4shared.com. Well, not a song, but THE WHOLE ALBUM! I downloaded the 'Danger' track, but not really impressed by it. Then, I downloaded the 'War of Hormone' and I got really addicted to it. The third time I revisit that webpage, I downloaded ALL SONGS FROM THAT ALBUM. Really, really addicted. That's the first time I downloaded all songs from an album in a short time.<br />
<br />
When the school session starts, I starts getting close to one of my senior (actually, just a year older than me) and talked about K-POP. And unknowingly, I really want to talk about BTS, since she's already an ARMY that time. It's so fun! I wish to meet her again, but it seems impossible...<br />
<br />
Until I moved to a new school on 10th February, still not telling my close friend in Salaping about BTS, suddenly I ignored BTS once again. Focused on EXO, but when I heard that BTS released a new album, I quickly downloaded the whole album (I downloaded it but I didn't hear the songs from the album at all bcuz of EXO's comeback). Then I met a new friend, who's also an EXO-L and we became close (just bcuz of E-POP. Thanks, E-POP!). I found out that she's also an ARMY (and that time I also figured what kind of 'ARMY' the netizens talked about on FB). I heard their song, 'I NEED U', but since it's a slow song, I'm not interested at all.<br />
<br />
When BTS came to KK, my friend got crazy and I helped her to find any information about them in KK. I searched for some pictures and posted it to FB so that she can saw it too. (J-Hope is disturbingly funny that time, with that snakeu). Even though they came to KK, I don't fall to them easily. Well, at least I knew some of the member's names... Jungkook (I knew him even before the DANGER era, actually, due to his popularity), J-Hope, and when I heard about Rap Monster, I thought "I think I've heard his name before..." but I ignored it. It's actually an honor that they came to Kota Kinabalu, since usually foreigner idols only came to Kuala Lumpur for concerts or fanmeeting. They came to KK for a schedule, and 'rest, enjoying their holiday' at the same time. They even go to LOK KAWI! Somehow, I felt proud for Sabahan. Guys, you are very lucky to be a Sabahan! But what's sad is... the earthquake... It's the most scary moment (and exciting since I never feel it before) in my life!<br />
<br />
Just in May/June (I don't remember well), I finally decided to listen to all songs from the album. And damn, 'DOPE' sounds so good and it catches my attention! It's a waste that I didn't hear to it earlier. It's a jam along with 'Hold Me Tight', 'Converse High' and 'Fun Boyz'. Just then, BTS dropped official photo teasers for their upcoming comeback (and we really thought it was for Part 2 of their previous album). Rap Monster is the first one to be revealed, and since that I collected each member's till the end. No, the first one is the hand... but I ignore it till Rap Monster's teaser appeared. And guess what, by following the updates from their official Facebook Page (I don't know when did I liked the page, anyway), I learned about which one is this and that. I hated Rap Monster, because his face reminds me of someone that I don't like the most. And Jimin, because being too hot and popular. LOL. Even though he really looks so handsome and looks like GD in that teaser.<br />
<br />
I'm late for the new MV... but when I finally watch it, I thought "OMG! MY FAV SONG FROM THE ALBUM!" and get very hyped!! 'I Need U' is too emotional, but this 'DOPE' MV is energetic! The dance, the rhythm, the melody, the RAPS, Jimin's high note and Jin's voice... I THINK I'VE FINALLY FALL IN LOVE WITH THE GROUP! And shockingly, even though I hate Rap Monster, I suddenly went crazy over his raps (and voice in the beginning) among the other rappers. It's weird that I've fall into Rap Monster's charisma when Jungkook is the most popular member and Jin is the most handsome member, when Jimin is the hottest member, J-Hope is the cutest and hypest member and V is the most unique and have a very soulful voice, and Suga is also a very good and talented rapper and is very swag (which is my style)... but I didn't fall easily, though. I think it's all started after I watched them on Weekly Idol, Bangtan Bomb, and read the translations of their songs ESPECIALLY Rap Monster's rap.<br />
<br />
I finally figured out that Rap Monster is good in English, and I found out that he is good for my motivation to not give up. He is my inspiration! Even when I found out that he's a pervert but all that important for me is he makes me positive (except for that curses), he is a very responsible leader, and I could understand his feelings when he said life is getting hard, he felt like gave up, he felt so empty, etc. because I'm facing that too. He is a professional, I got a feeling I should learn something from him. His songs, mixtapes, gives me inspiration. It feels like I've finally found someone that can reflect me almost... perfectly, but not completely. I'll try to take only the good thing about him. He is a genius?? I don't care! Whether a genius or not, his maturity level of his mental (lol my words), his perspectives, the way he was thinking, his weird personality, anything positives from him... I take that as my inspiration.<br />
<br />
That's why... when I read the article,, I cried unknowingly, and only realize when my tear drop falls onto my lap. I can't describe it, but it's not sad, it's not amazing, it's not funny, it's not happy... it's an understanding feeling, I think. Whoever can understand what I feel, I hope we can talk to each other, discussing about our curiousness and doubts, finding the answers of all questions that's playing in our mind. I can't even read till the end of the interview, only 1 and a half page (not even a half, actually) and quickly close the magazine and swept my tears by using my blanket (cuz I couldn't find my handkerchief or tissues and blanket is the only thing that's close to my hand to reach). I'm not going to take a risk to cry. I can cry next time, but not now. It'll be obvious. Too obvious when I cry.<br />
<br />
That's why... since a long time ago, I dreamed of going to another place to learn. Same dream, but different reasons. Since I live with a perfect family, given all that I need, and mostly faced no problem at all, I wanted to feel how... how the others who lived unfortunately like me be like. I know, I would found an answer for that. Life is... A POOP. (lol. kidding.).Life is complicated, a journey, a learning process, a puzzle, a maze, a challenge, the time that God had given us to change to be better and faithful. And for me, life is a test. You failed, you learn from the past and try to pass in the future. It's complicated. It's subjective. Too much definitions. Too much speculations.<br />
<br />
To all this while, I wanted to thank to myself for being tough, for being positive and avoid all the negativity. To not get involved in bad things.<br />
<br />
This... is... the... first... time... I... cried... because... of... an... article.<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
I'm being dramatic, right? Hahaha~~ I'm sorry. I didn't mean to be dramatic at all.
