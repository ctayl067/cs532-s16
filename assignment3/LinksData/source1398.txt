<p class="introduction">St Catherine's is Oxford's youngest undergraduate college and we bring a fresh, contemporary feel to the Oxford conference experience - 800 years of University history cast in a modern mould.</p>
<p>During College vacations we can offer you the perfect venue for  residential conferences of up to 250 delegates (using one main lecture space).  Our dedicated conference spaces  also welcome training days, day conferences and meetings/events for up to 80 daytime participants during term time.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0;">Our buildings and facilities provide a wonderful combination for any event. They include:<br>
  <br>
</p>
<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;"><span style="font-weight: bold"><a href="working/index.php">*</a></span><a href="working/index.php"><strong>&nbsp; Purpose-built and varied lecture theatres, seminar rooms and office&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</strong></a></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;"><a href="working/index.php"><strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	space&nbsp;complete with audio visual equipment;&nbsp;</strong></a><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;"><strong><a href="sleeping/index.php">*&nbsp; Circa 250 purpose-built, modern ensuite bedrooms during vacations;</a></strong></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;"><strong><a href="eating/index.php">*&nbsp; The largest dining hall of the Oxford Colleges - Grade 1 Listed, </a></strong></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;"><a href="http://www.catzconferences.co.uk/eating/"><strong> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong><a href="eating/index.php">designed&nbsp;by Arne Jacobsen and seating up to 380 guests;</strong></a></strong></a></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0;"><strong><a href="eating/index.php">*&nbsp; In-house kitchen and waiting teams who pride themselves on fresh, high <br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; quality food and exceptional service.</a></strong></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0;">&nbsp;</p>
