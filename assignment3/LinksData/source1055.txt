	<div class="entry-content clearfix">
		<p><span style="color: #333399;"><em><a href="http://livandluv.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/2014.01.21-62.jpeg"><img class="alignnone wp-image-39" src="http://livandluv.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/2014.01.21-62.jpeg" alt="pregnancy" width="1584" height="1057" /></a>&#8220;The most beautiful things in the world cannot be seen or touched, they are felt with the heart.&#8221; &#8211; The Little Prince </em></span></p>
<p>Liv And Luv Skincare for Mom &amp; Baby was created to be gentle on your baby&#8217;s skin and gentle on the earth. At Liv and Luv, we are selling products with only Certified Natural &amp; Organic ingredients. With so many organic skincare brands, I, as a mother, tried them all! I always believed the label which said they were organic and safe until I started reading the ingredients closer and realized how I got fooled! Being in skincare for 10 years as a medical esthetician and consultant for dermatologists and spas, I have always cared about what I put on my skin. But it wasn&#8217;t until the day I gave birth to my beautiful fair skinned red head son that I realized how important it is to a new baby&#8217;s gentle skin. My son Olivier &#8220;LIV&#8221; didn&#8217;t take after the dark skin of his parents but he was crazy like his dad, and a lover like his Mama -Tephy &#8220;LUV<em>&#8220;</em>  (it&#8217;s a childhood nickname!) That&#8217;s how we became Liv &amp; Luv.  I have seen and treated all types of skin conditions, but having this tiny ginger baby with porcelain skin was a whole new challenge! He also began suffering with bouts of eczema which made me even more mindful of what I used on his precious little body! With my love of the ocean, outdoors and sunshine being passed onto Liv, I knew I needed something amazingly effective yet natural to keep him both protected and safe. Using edible grade &amp; organic ingredients that are completely natural, you&#8217;ll know that you&#8217;re only spreading the best on your little ones and on yourself. The best part is that you can actually read the ingredients and understand what they mean. The most gentle line for the whole family to use, from the beach to the bath!</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">*Coming from Hawaii, aloha is important to how we live our life. Aloha is harmony, helpfulness, humility and unity. To us it&#8217;s our Ohana (family) first, meaningful friendships, counting our blessings, and giving to others. 10% of all profits are donated to local charities for babies and children who can use some aloha in their world. </span></p>
<p>Thank you for spreading the Aloha too!</p>
