We Are Looking For The Next RAW

   [13]Leave a reply

   The business of distributing branded [14]cannabis products (such as
   rolling papers) is dominated by [15]RAW. Consumers are well aware of
   the company, their products and their relationship with rap superstar
   [16]Wiz Khalifa The company is a standout in the cannabis distribution
   industry and it is a great [17]role model for others intent on entering
   the field. These guys have a ton of [18]SKUs and they are dominant in
   their space. Although the company is private – cannabis industry
   players seem to believe that RAW  (which can be found at
   Rawthentic.com) is grossing $50 million a year (or more). Our team
   works with investors who are interested in speaking  to you if you are
   distributing cannabis products in the US or [19]Canada. Please feel
   free to reach to us by email @ [20]contact@startuphangar.com or on our
   [21]Twitter handle @ Startuphangar.
