        <h1 id="homeh1">We are delighted to welcome you on this great adventure</h1>
        <div class="leftcol w49">
          
<div id="node-1" class=""><div class="node-inner">

  
  
  
  
  <div class="content">
    <p>We are delighted to welcome you to this great adventure - an Anglican church planted from <a href="http://htb.org.uk" target="_blank">Holy Trinity Brompton</a> in 2009, building a fun community in one of the most iconic and recognised buildings in the heart of Brighton, with a vision to play our part in the re-evangelisation of the nations and the transformation of society. Feel free to join us at one of our 5<a href="services-0">&nbsp;Sunday services</a>.</p>
<p>If you would like to explore the meaning of life in an informal environment, we would love to invite you to join the next <a href="alpha">Alpha course</a>.</p>
<p>We have&nbsp;<a href="join-team">Teams</a>&nbsp;which are&nbsp;great places to connect and to see the vision of St Peter&rsquo;s alive in Brighton. During the week we get together in&nbsp;<a href="http://stpetersbrighton.org/hubs">Hubs</a>&nbsp;-&nbsp;fortnightly gatherings of anything between 15-25 people, for food, friends, teaching, worship and prayer - the ideal way to feel like you belong.</p>
<p>For anything else, please check out the <a href="http://www.facebook.com/pages/St-Peters-Brighton/140638266459" target="_blank">Facebook</a> page, and don&#39;t hesitate to <a href="contact">contact us</a></p>
<p>With best wishes - Archie and Sam</p>
