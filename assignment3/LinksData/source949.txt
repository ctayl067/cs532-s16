</h3>

<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-2089600799720804182' itemprop='articleBody'>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Blue
Chip GuacaMollie<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-A9TgEkBA3D4/VraBRNMF_YI/AAAAAAAAAns/AIECyRhCmic/s1600/untitled-1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="280" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-A9TgEkBA3D4/VraBRNMF_YI/AAAAAAAAAns/AIECyRhCmic/s320/untitled-1.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">As
an uprooted Texan living in Maine for more than 25 years, I've learned that if
I get a hankerin' for Tex-Mex, I'd better make it myself. So ... here's my
"Mainer" variation on that healthful green favorite otherwise known
and Guacamole.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Ingredients:</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br />
2-3 ripe avocados, peeled with pits removed and set aside*</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-zm1mAeijKA4/VraBQ-CyOLI/AAAAAAAAAno/uVKWRMh00p4/s1600/untitled-2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="262" src="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-zm1mAeijKA4/VraBQ-CyOLI/AAAAAAAAAno/uVKWRMh00p4/s320/untitled-2.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-language: AR-SA; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; mso-fareast-language: EN-US;"><!--[if gte vml 1]><v:shapetype id="_x0000_t75"

</v:shape><![endif]--><!--[if !vml]--><!--[endif]--></span><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br />
1 small onion, finely chopped<br />
1 tsp. Worchestershire sauce or freshly squeezed lemon or lime juice</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-kxUw67PiLd0/VraBRCpacUI/AAAAAAAAAnw/mOqFJ1Juqu4/s1600/untitled-3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="267" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-kxUw67PiLd0/VraBRCpacUI/AAAAAAAAAnw/mOqFJ1Juqu4/s320/untitled-3.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br />
1 tsp. ground cumin <br />
Sea salt to taste<br />
Cucumber, peeled and sliced<br />
Fresh baby spinach leaves or mixed salad greens, washed and spun dry<br />
Blue corn chips<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Garnishes:<br />
1 ripe tomato, peeled, seeded and finely chopped or minced**<br />
Chopped ripe olives<br />
Fresh cilantro<br />
Sour cream<br />
Shredded cheddar or Monterey jack cheese<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">In
a small-medium mixing bowl, smash avocados with a fork to moderately lumpy
consistency.</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-KuD2Hw6D6p8/VraBRGDCn0I/AAAAAAAAAn0/p0GIjH52Cc0/s1600/untitled-4.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="240" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-KuD2Hw6D6p8/VraBRGDCn0I/AAAAAAAAAn0/p0GIjH52Cc0/s320/untitled-4.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>

Add and mix in onion, W</span><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">orchestershire sauce or citrus juice, cumin and salt.</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
**Tomato may also be mixed in with avocados, onion, etc.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Serve
on a bed or sliced cukes, spinach or salad greens. Spike with corn ships and
add your choice of garnishes. <o:p></o:p></span></div>

<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://jewelryonetsyteam.blogspot.com/2016/02/whats-on-my-bench_13.html'>
What's on My Bench???
</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-763783732926163880' itemprop='articleBody'>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 12.0pt;">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Some of our team members live close enough to get together
in real life.&nbsp;&nbsp;I&#8217;m one of those lucky team members and so is
<a href="https://www.etsy.com/people/earthenergygemstones?ref=owner_profile_leftnav" target="_blank">Gloria </a>of <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/EarthEnergyGemstones?ref=pr_shop_more" target="_blank">EarthEnergyGemstones</a>!&nbsp;&nbsp;In true JET fashion, we&nbsp;decided to get together and
play.&nbsp;&nbsp;Gloria was interested in learning how to create bezels and set
stones in them.&nbsp;&nbsp;And a play date was had!!<br />
<br />
Fine silver bezel strip is most commonly used.&nbsp;&nbsp;It&#8217;s pre-formed and
comes in various widths to encircle various sized of stones.&nbsp;&nbsp;Stones
cut en cabochon&nbsp;are most commonly set, but you can also set faceted gems.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 1.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Select
a stone to set.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 2.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Measure
your bezel height.&nbsp;&nbsp;Hold the wire next to the stone.&nbsp;&nbsp;It
should be about 1 mm taller than the juncture where the stone&#8217;s wall starts to
form a curve.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 3.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Wrap
your wire around the stone and mark where the wire starts to
overlap.&nbsp;&nbsp;Cut just past the mark with a pair of
snips. &nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-klWixM3c2bM/VrfPv0QdP6I/AAAAAAAAAoQ/8ZUUquooI_E/s1600/1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="275" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-klWixM3c2bM/VrfPv0QdP6I/AAAAAAAAAoQ/8ZUUquooI_E/s320/1.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 4.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Form
the wire circle to closely fit the stone and make sure edges line up
exactly.&nbsp;&nbsp;No Gaps!<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 5.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Dip
your silver in a firescale prevention solution (2 parts denatured alcohol to 1
part boric acid crystals).&nbsp;&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 6.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Set on
your fire safe surface. &nbsp;Set the bezel such that the seam sits directly on
top of a piece of hard solder chip paillon. &nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-OddLnuJcW98/VrfPvyiyMwI/AAAAAAAAAoU/TabEoxN2Q4A/s1600/2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="308" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-OddLnuJcW98/VrfPvyiyMwI/AAAAAAAAAoU/TabEoxN2Q4A/s320/2.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 7.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Paint
flux on seam.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 8.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Heat
with torch until solder chip melts and closes the seam.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 9.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Pickle
and rinse.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 10.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Cut sheet
silver to make a back plate for the bezel. &nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-J13gWuBpqh8/VrfPvgAGvWI/AAAAAAAAAoM/SB8zxvP_Uqw/s1600/3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="306" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-J13gWuBpqh8/VrfPvgAGvWI/AAAAAAAAAoM/SB8zxvP_Uqw/s320/3.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 11.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Repeat
solder steps from above to solder bezel onto the back plate using several
medium solder chips placed inside the circle where the&nbsp;bezel touches the
back plate. &nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-jw78M52aI6g/VrfPwCFA1aI/AAAAAAAAAoY/8r7SIVWh424/s1600/4.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="283" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-jw78M52aI6g/VrfPwCFA1aI/AAAAAAAAAoY/8r7SIVWh424/s320/4.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 12.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Pickle
and rinse<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 13.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Using the
snips, cut excess sheet silver and sand until smooth and round.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 12.0pt;">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br />
<br />
Now we have a bezel we can set a stone in.&nbsp;&nbsp;You can do just about
anything with a bezel.&nbsp;&nbsp;You can make into a ring, and earring, or a
pendant.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Today&nbsp;we&#8217;ve made a ring for our little moonstone
cab.&nbsp;&nbsp;We&#8217;ve placed our ring into a vice in order to push the bezel
over the stone to secure it.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 1.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>With a
bezel pusher, push the bezel horizontally around the stone in a North, South,
East, West fashion so that the stone is in the center.&nbsp;&nbsp;Continue all
the way around the circle.&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-u8OYn8Q_lI4/VrfPwMBryzI/AAAAAAAAAoc/Fsv8h0RhOvU/s1600/5.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="240" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-u8OYn8Q_lI4/VrfPwMBryzI/AAAAAAAAAoc/Fsv8h0RhOvU/s320/5.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 2.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Now
push the bezel down at the top edge, holding your bezel pusher at about a 50
degree angle.&nbsp;&nbsp;It should be smooth and flush against your&nbsp;stone.&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-4f_Sog-Fa60/VrfPwTxuq8I/AAAAAAAAAog/6OHukU7FMI8/s1600/6.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="320" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-4f_Sog-Fa60/VrfPwTxuq8I/AAAAAAAAAog/6OHukU7FMI8/s320/6.jpg" width="295" /></a></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; 3.<span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Using a
steel burnisher, smooth out the silver to polish the edge of the bezel.&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-QsAR-TatWz4/VrfPwUX-BRI/AAAAAAAAAok/k6BC_o3Ndx0/s1600/7.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="267" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-QsAR-TatWz4/VrfPwUX-BRI/AAAAAAAAAok/k6BC_o3Ndx0/s320/7.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<br /></div>
<br />
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><span class="apple-tab-span">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>&#8226; Ta-Da!! &nbsp;</span><o:p></o:p></div>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://jewelryonetsyteam.blogspot.com/2016/02/cooking-with-jets.html'>
Cooking with the JETs
</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-3907946266676758793' itemprop='articleBody'>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">&nbsp;LAMB SOUP</span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Well let me just say first about how much I love my electric
pressure cooker.&nbsp; When you are busy taking care of the family and would
like to cook fast healthy meals, an electric pressure cooker is a very big help!&nbsp;
It's like a slow cooker and you can use many of your slow cooker recipes, but
everything cooks a lot a lot faster!&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-7QZksWRDcPY/VrMD3N-HNjI/AAAAAAAAAmY/CzE5kz4SszE/s1600/IMG_20151230_103240.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="320" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-7QZksWRDcPY/VrMD3N-HNjI/AAAAAAAAAmY/CzE5kz4SszE/s320/IMG_20151230_103240.jpg" width="240" /></a></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<br /></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Ingredients:<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<br /></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">1-1.5 pound diced lamb (I usually purchase the lamb shoulder
chops because that's what I have available at my super market and dice them up)<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">2 yellow onions chopped<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">1 stalk celery chopped (optional)<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">1 tbsp fresh or dry parsley chopped<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">1 tbsp fresh cilantro chopped<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">1 stick cinnamon or 1 tsp ground cinnamon&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">5 strands saffron crumbled&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">1/4 tsp ground ginger<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Salt &amp; ground black pepper<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">2 boxes (each approximately 32 oz) of chicken broth&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">1 can (15 oz) of garbanzo beans (chickpeas)<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">2 cups red lentils<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">1 can (28 oz) diced tomatoes or diced fresh tomatoes<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<br /></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Some fresh cilantro and sour cream for garnish&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<br /></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">To make the soup:&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<br /></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">

<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;">Finding the Flash in Labradorite</span></div>
<span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><br /></span>
<br />
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;">Labradorite is one of my very favorite gemstones to work
with. Its mysterious colors that appear from such an unassuming neutral
background are so beautiful, and surprising. It got its name from the Labrador
Peninsula in Canada where it was found by Moravian missionaries in 1770 in the
early 19<sup>th</sup>&nbsp;Century. The local Inuit people knew about it for
much longer and believed that Labradorite fell from the frozen fire of the
Northern Lights. They believed &nbsp;this ordinary looking stone could transform
into the extraordinary, since it holds the energy and the colors of the Aurora
Borealis.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<br /></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;">Labradorite is a mineral of the plagioclase feldspar group
with a hardness of 6-6.5. The amazing play of color in the stone is known as
&#8220;labradorescence&#8221;. The background color of Labradorite is usually gray to gray-green, brownish-gray, dark blue-gray or black. Since it is made up of aggregate
layers that refract light, iridescent flashes in the &nbsp;blue, green, gold or
red range can be seen varying with the angle of light. There are rarer
varieties of Labradorite that include Golden Labradorite which is a transparent
gold or champagne color, and Spectrolite which can display the whole color
spectrum and especially a vivid electric blue. This variety was discovered in
Finland in the 1940s.<span style="background: white;"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<br /></div>
<div class="MsoNormal">
<span style="background: white;"><span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;">What&#8217;s on my bench today are some labradorite
cabochons which need to be set. The challenge in working with labradorite is to
find which direction you see the flash of color and set the stone so the color
can best be seen. I find that &nbsp;by &#8220;playing with them&#8221; and moving the
stones around I can see how the light affects them. That will help me determine
how to use these cabochons - in hanging settings, or in horizontal settings.<o:p></o:p></span></span><br />

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://jewelryonetsyteam.blogspot.com/2016/02/one-of-my-favorites.html'>
One of My Favorites
</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-5130797603546238301' itemprop='articleBody'>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 13.5pt;">
<span style="color: #333333;"><br />
<br /><span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;">
Hi my name is <a href="https://www.etsy.com/people/DivineSpiritCreation?ref=owner_profile_leftnav" target="_blank">Lora</a> of <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/DivineSpiritCreation?ref=pr_shop_more" target="_blank">DivineSpiritCreations</a> and designer of Pyrex Jewelry in
addition to other silver and gemstone jewelry. But I wanted to talk to you
today about one of my favorite tools - my vintage muffin tins. <br />
<br />
That's right muffin tins. I use them in my design process to hold materials as
I create - the mini tins are the perfect depth to hold stones and findings and
they're all right in front of you.</span></span><br />
<span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><br /></span></span>

<span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;">&nbsp;I used the larger and decorative vintage
tins to hold items that are "in process" since at a certain point I
like to assembly line certain things.</span></span><br />
<span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><br /></span></span>

<span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;">&nbsp;I've even used my muffin tins for travel

<span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><br /></span></span>

<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Z6OQ8emZojo/VrOPBQnW1NI/AAAAAAAAAnQ/CH6gWfsZrKI/s1600/2016-02-04%2B11.14.42.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="240" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Z6OQ8emZojo/VrOPBQnW1NI/AAAAAAAAAnQ/CH6gWfsZrKI/s320/2016-02-04%2B11.14.42.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>

<span style="color: #333333;"><span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><br /></span></span>
<span style="color: #333333; line-height: 13.5pt;"><span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;">So easily found at yard sales and thrift shops at a
