   [61]pregnancy “The most beautiful things in the world cannot be seen or
   touched, they are felt with the heart.” – The Little Prince

   Liv And Luv Skincare for Mom & Baby was created to be gentle on your
   baby’s skin and gentle on the earth. At Liv and Luv, we are selling
   products with only Certified Natural & Organic ingredients. With so
   many organic skincare brands, I, as a mother, tried them all! I always
   believed the label which said they were organic and safe until I
   started reading the ingredients closer and realized how I got fooled!
   Being in skincare for 10 years as a medical esthetician and consultant
   for dermatologists and spas, I have always cared about what I put on my
   skin. But it wasn’t until the day I gave birth to my beautiful fair
   skinned red head son that I realized how important it is to a new
   baby’s gentle skin. My son Olivier “LIV” didn’t take after the dark
   skin of his parents but he was crazy like his dad, and a lover like his
   Mama -Tephy “LUV“  (it’s a childhood nickname!) That’s how we became
   Liv & Luv.  I have seen and treated all types of skin conditions, but
   having this tiny ginger baby with porcelain skin was a whole new
   challenge! He also began suffering with bouts of eczema which made me
   even more mindful of what I used on his precious little body! With my
   love of the ocean, outdoors and sunshine being passed onto Liv, I knew
   I needed something amazingly effective yet natural to keep him both
   protected and safe. Using edible grade & organic ingredients that are
   completely natural, you’ll know that you’re only spreading the best on
   your little ones and on yourself. The best part is that you can
   actually read the ingredients and understand what they mean. The most
   gentle line for the whole family to use, from the beach to the bath!

   *Coming from Hawaii, aloha is important to how we live our life. Aloha
   is harmony, helpfulness, humility and unity. To us it’s our Ohana
   (family) first, meaningful friendships, counting our blessings, and
   giving to others. 10% of all profits are donated to local charities for
   babies and children who can use some aloha in their world.

   Thank you for spreading the Aloha too!

                  Liv & Luv
