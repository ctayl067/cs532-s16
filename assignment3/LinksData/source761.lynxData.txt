   Believing in Your Dreams
   Staying true to yourself and not being afraid to take a step forward is
   the secret to success and the main reason why we started this business.
   The more you realize your talent the more confident you become. Waking
   up every morning and knowing you can make a difference in a persons
   life is the biggest reward anyone can receive. We are mainly focused on
   children's clothing right now. The inspiration behind our First Step
   Clothing line is, growth, tenacity, and individuality. We carry a wide
   selection of sizes from 3m to 5T, depending on the style. We can also
   personalize your order if you want a particular detail or embellishment
   added to it.
