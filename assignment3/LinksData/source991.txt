        <p>Thanks for stopping by. I write novels about people falling in love, among other things. </p>

        <p>My first book had a very pink cover and was called <a href="http://www.loreleimathias.com/novels-soic.html">Step on it, Cupid</a>. Then a year later came <a href="http://www.loreleimathias.com/novels-lfw.html">Lost For Words</a>; a romantic comedy set in a publishing house.</p>

        <p>Since then I've been busy writing a few other things - everything from short films to adverts, from poems to post-it notes... but finally, the difficult third novel has arrived! I bring you, <a href="novels-ridh.html">Reader, I Dumped Him</a> - A love story about break-ups.</p>
	        <p>It’s about four Londoners at the fag-end of their twenties. It celebrates what becomes of the consciously-uncoupled, when they happen to all consciously-uncouple at the exact same time…. end up forming a slightly pathetic club, and fall head over heels with each other. <a href="novels-ridh.html">You can read and watch all about it here.</a></p>

        <p>For now, feel free to have a look around... watch <a href="http://loreleimathias.com/play.html">some movies</a>....read the <a href="http://loreleimathias.com/whats-new.html">news</a>... or look at <a href="https://loreleimathias.wordpress.com/">my blog</a>.</p>
